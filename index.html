Meet my family!

This is my Mejala Family. They are one of the reasons why I'm still here. They are the ones who I grew up with. I love these people and I know they love me as well, and I thank God for giving me this family.

From left to right, My uncle Marlon, My grandfather Numeriano, My sibling Samantha, My cousin Hyacinth, My grandmother Sittirabia, and My mother, Matrimar.

My Family History

    In our family, we have a total of 9 members. My grandfather's name is Numeriano Mejala, and his wife, Sittirabia Lahuran Mejala, are my mother's parents. As you can see, my grandmothers name is a bit different, its because she's a native from Mindanao. They have 3 children named, Marlon, Matrimar, and Mark. My uncle Marlon doesn't have a family of his own and he's currently at Metro Manila. My mom, Matrimar aka Mhatty, is at Tarlac with her husband, Ace, and my sister Samantha. While they stay at Tarlac City, I and Kiana currently stays with our grandparents. My other uncle, Mark, have a daughter named Hyacinth, which is also here with us.
